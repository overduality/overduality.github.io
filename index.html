Dental Age Estimation Using Deep Learning

Automated age prediction from panoramic dental radiographs using EfficientNet-B3 and CBAM attention mechanism. Achieved 0.690 years MAE on Indonesian pediatric population, outperforming traditional manual methods while providing clinical interpretability through Grad-CAM visualization.
